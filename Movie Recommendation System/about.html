WELCOME TO THE WORLD OF MOVIES
Here you get ratings, trailers and even the link of all the movies.You will get an ordered list of the movies according to the bluckbuster level.
Functionality of the website:
(1) You have good movies at the top and you can search for your desired movies.
(2) On hovering one of the video give you the overview page
(3) On clicking know more your overlay window opens to present you the youtube videos.
(4) You can click over the tags present at the top and can search for a movie with the particular combination of features/tags.
(5) You can sign up in the bottom and your data will be printed in the console.
Once you click on Get Started! You will see main web page of movie recommendation. Now if you want to search movie of your choice then write the movie name in Search Your Desired Movie and Press Enter key of your laptop. Soon you will see on the screen same and similar movies after hovering on every movie, there are overview about movies and after clicking on know more button you will get to know more about that.
If you want to watch tags type of movies which is already taged on the top of screen. Once you click on any one of the taged movie for example Action, Adventure, Animation, Comedy etc. You will observe on the screen similar to the taged, movies are present and Important thing is that a Clear x button same as taged button in the second line right side. To move from one taged type to another taged type (for example: Adventure to Animation), first click on Clear x button the move on next any tag. This will give you better user experience, otherwise it will not work perfectly if you violate the rule.
